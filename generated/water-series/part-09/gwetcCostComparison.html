What gets shared — and why it matters:
ROWOne negotiation instead of five. One set of easements, permits, and land agreements.
SurveyingOne geotechnical and topographic survey covers all five systems.
GradingOne earthworks mobilization. Equipment moves once, not five times.
SecurityOne security perimeter, one monitoring network, one response team.
MaintenanceOne road, one crew, one set of tools serves all five systems.
